Our Story

Valentina Icecream was founded by two best friends who wanted to share their love for ice cream with the world. Our ice cream is made with natural ingredients and is always hand-crafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality. We believe that ice cream should be enjoyed as a treat, but it should also be made with ingredients you can trust.

Our Flavors

Strawberry

Our strawberry ice cream is made with fresh, juicy strawberries and sweet cream. It's the perfect balance of sweet and tart.

Chocolate

Our chocolate ice cream is made with rich, dark cocoa and creamy milk. It's the perfect indulgence for chocolate lovers.

Vanilla

Our vanilla ice cream is made with real vanilla beans and fresh cream. It's a classic flavor that never goes out of style.
